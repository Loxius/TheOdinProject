Tinga Recipe

Ingredients

  • 5 lb chicken thighs
  • 1 onion
  • Chicken Bouillon
  • Tomato Sauce 16oz
  • Ice Berg Lettuce
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 5 Chipotle Peppers
  • Sour Cream
  • Tostadas
  • Avocado
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Cumin Powder
  • Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil chicken with onions and chicken bouillon
  2. Slice onions and lettuce while chicken is boiling
  3. Shred chicken after it's cooked
  4. Blend boiled onions, 3 garlic cloves, 5 chipotle peppers, some of the liquid, more chicken bouillon, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, black pepper and tomato sauce
  5. In a pan add oil and cook the onions until soften and then put in the sauce and the shredded chicken in the pan with the onions, and cook until done.
